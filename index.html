Centuries-Old Craft Traditions

Four pillars of Gilgit-Baltistan artistry passed down through generations.

🧵

Iraghi Embroidery

Vibrant cross-stitch patterns on Hunza caps, shawls, and waistcoats — each motif tells a story of mountain life.

🪵

Walnut Woodcarving

Hunza's dark walnut is hand-carved into boxes, furniture, and decorative panels with floral relief work.

🧶

Sharma Weaving

Goat and sheep wool rugs woven on traditional looms with bold tribal red, black, and white patterns.

💎

Lapis & Silver

Celestial-blue lapis lazuli from regional mines set in handcrafted sterling silver tribal jewelry.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering mountain crafts from Gilgit-Baltistan.

How do I place an order?

Browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also message us directly on WhatsApp for a personal shopping experience. We'll confirm stock and delivery details within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EasyPaisa, JazzCash, bank transfer (HBL/Meezan), Cash on Delivery across Pakistan, and WhatsApp orders. International customers can pay via bank transfer after confirming on WhatsApp.

How long does delivery take?

Orders within Gilgit-Baltistan arrive in 3–5 days. Major cities in Pakistan (Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi) take 5–10 business days. International shipping timelines are shared individually via WhatsApp.

Are all products genuinely handmade?

Yes — every item is sourced directly from local artisans in Hunza, Skardu, and Gilgit. We personally verify each craftsperson and their traditional techniques before listing their work.

Can I return or exchange an item?

We offer a 7-day return window for unused items in original condition. Custom or personalized orders cannot be returned. Contact us on WhatsApp with your order reference to start a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to the UK, USA, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and many other countries. Message us on WhatsApp with your location and we'll provide shipping costs and estimated delivery time.
